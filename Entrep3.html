Recommended Workouts

Bench Press

Target: Chest, Triceps, Shoulders

Squats

Target: Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Glutes

Deadlifts

Target: Back, Hamstrings, Core

Pull-ups

Target: Back, Biceps, Shoulders

Cautions and Tips

  • Always warm up before starting your workout
  • Use proper form to prevent injuries
  • Gradually increase weight and reps over time
  • Allow for adequate rest and recovery between workouts
  • Maintain a balanced diet rich in protein and nutrients
  • Stay hydrated before, during, and after workouts
  • Consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new exercise program
